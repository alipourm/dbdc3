Information for Sponsor Recruitment

Dialogue Breakdown Detection Challenge 3（DBDC3)

For DBDC3 to be held in DSTC6, we are recruiting sponsor companies that can approve of the purpose of this challenge. Specifically, we would like to ask for your support as sponsorship, for collecting dialogue data through crowdsourcing and annotating broken dialogue labels. In addition, this data expansion associates with an international movement on chat bots WOCHAT and a live competition held at NIPS2017 Conversational Intelligence Challenge (CIC).

A number of dialogue researchers are expected to participate in this event. By sponsoring, we believe that it can be useful for your appealing products and services, and recruiting activities.

For the personnel of companies that can apply to sponsors, please read the application guidelines below and fill in the required items, contact us during the period. We greatly appreciate your cooperation.


About

  • Date
    Monday December 04 -- Saturday December 09, 2017
  • Place
    Long Beach Convention Center, Long Beach
    DSTC 6 will be conducted as NIPS2017 workshop (12/4-9) or an event of NIPS2017.
  • Reference
    DBDC1(Japanese), DBDC2(Japanese)

Reception period and Sponsor fee

  • Reception period
    Until Monday, August 14, 2017
  • Sponsor fee
    One bid 250,000 yen (Sponsor classification is not established)

Required items

Please fill in the required items, contact us during the period.

  • Name of the sponsor company, name of the person in charge, department name, e-mail address, telephone number
  • Appointment of quote and invoice
  • Company name (Name displayed on Web page or brochure)
  • Logo image and URL (Please send only one, we will link from logo image)

Sponsorship benefits

  • Logo
    Your company's logo will be posted on the web page of DBDC3 and presentation materials on the day. The order of posting is basically the order of application. For the logo you can attach a link to one desired link destination.
  • Sponsor talk
    Companies of sponsors can talk by poster or rostrum in DBDC3. The presentation time is about 5 to 10 minutes by rostrum, or all the time in the poster session.
  • Leaflet
    You can place leaflets that participants and auditors can freely pick up in the venue of the workshop.
  • Credit
    We will credit your company as the creator of the data.

Usage of Sponsor Fee

All the funds you support will be used for making DBDC3 development and evaluation data (Japanese and English). If you support a lot, it will be possible to create as much data. In addition, the created data will be shared resources that can be used for research and development (both commercial and non-commercial).

In addition to using DBDC3, collected data will be used even after DBDC4. It is expected that the data created in this operation will be continuously used in dialogue system research.
